Our History

It all started when 3 friends who decided to get together to create a small company called "tigres btg", the idea arose thanks to one of the colleagues presenting an idea as an innovative product, to the colleagues they liked this idea, so it was thought to sell it with the purpose of transmitting emotions and memories. For them it was not a simple stuffed animal but it was to remember those moments together and to be able to have something to remembering it, we chose to choose a tiger as a stuffed animal that was small and had that aspect that when having it in your arms made you remember your beautiful moments during your stay in high school this microenterprise managed to create something beautiful and unique to remember each person from btg who wanted to buy their tiger and with it embrace his memories.
